The Student Financial Universe

Smart budgeting, shared expenses, and habit nudges for students — built from real survey pain points.

Open Prototype View Code

Problem

Students struggle with peer-pressure spending, lack of financial knowledge, and tracking expenses. Many also juggle loans, rent sharing, and limited access to tools.

Solution

FinX combines: auto-budgeting, group expense splits, micro-nudges, goal-based saving, and SOS emergency planning — all mobile-first.

Why Now

Students widely use apps like Splitwise/Jupiter/Cred/Walnut; FinX unifies core workflows and adds guidance built on survey-driven insights.

        Survey Insights

        We synthesized 1,000 student responses to shape features. Common themes included: peer-pressure spending, lack of financial knowledge, budgeting challenges, loans, rent sharing, and emergency planning.

        Dataset notes

        The attached CSV contains minor anomalies (e.g., a “Rural=COUNTIF(” artifact). The prototype ignores malformed tokens when summarizing insights client-side.
